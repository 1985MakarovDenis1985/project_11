MELROSE RESTAURANT
IN THE CETRE OF DNIPRO

MELROSE RESTAURANT USES HIGH QUALITY, FRESH, LOCALLY
SOURSED INGREDIENTS TO CREATE INNOVATIVE MODERN
UKRAINIAN DISHES.

Under Head Chef Serhiy Petrenko, who's doctrine is ‘from the gate to the plate’ or 'from the trawler to the plate', Melrose has built a
reputation among Dnipro residents for the excellence of its cuisine and the warmth of its staff.

Its fully air-conditioned cocktail bar and lounge area is also a popular gathering place for those drawn by its quiet sophistication
and contemporary wine, beer and cocktail list.

Melrose serves buffet breakfast and A La Carte lunch and dinner seven days.
